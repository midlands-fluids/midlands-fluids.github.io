Midlands Fluid Mechanics Meeting MFMM2024

Location: College Court, University of Leicester

Date: 2nd September 2024

The second edition of the Midlands Fluid Mechanics Meeting will be held at the University of Leicester on Monday 2nd September 2024.

This is a reminder that the Registration and Abstract Submission will close this coming Friday, 16th August 2024, at 11:59pm. Registration is FREE. If you have not already registered, please do so using the following form before this date. The meeting will be held at College Court, the University of Leicester’s Conference Centre, starting at approximately 9:30am and concluding by 5:30pm. The final schedule for the day will be confirmed shortly and we will distribute this to every participant before the meeting. Travel information on getting to College Court (including a useful map) can be found here.

Lunch and tea/coffee throughout the day will be provided, followed by an optional evening meal. For those of you who have signed up to attend the optional evening meal, we will also be circulating details of this once everything is confirmed.

We look forward to welcoming you to the University of Leicester for this year’s meeting.
