Midlands Fluid Mechanics Group

A warm welcome to the Midlands Fluid Mechanics Group!

The Midlands Fluid Mechanics Group (MFMG) unites academics across the Midlands who are engaged in fluid mechanics research. With a wealth of pioneering research being conducted in the region, our group provides a platform to share insights from our respective institutions and fosters the development of new academic collaborations.

Leadership Team: Dr. Chandan Bose (University of Birmingham), Dr. Paul Griffiths (Aston University), Dr. James Jewkes (University of Leicester).

Join the email list: midlands-fluidmechanics-group@jiscmail.ac.uk.
